Our Donation Plan

We donate food and shelter for the homeless families and also provide educational expenses for their children

Food For a Family

We provide food for the homeless and the people who live below the poverty line. People can come and distribute their donated food by themselves.

Shelter for a Family

We build shelters and buildings for the homeless. we also give them loans without any interest, so that they can build houses of their own.

Education for Children

We built many schools and colleges for the needy people and bear the educational expenses through our foundation and make their education free of cost.
